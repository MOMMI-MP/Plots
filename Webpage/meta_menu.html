Mouse strains:

Users can view box-plots for abundance of identified differentially abundant metabolites in each mouse strain.
Samples at gestational time points G15 and G19 were grouped together and compared to all the remaining time points
were grouped together. The PERMANOVA analysis was performed on those two groups for every individual feature.

The result obtained was an adjusted p-value table that describes the significance of separation between the groups for
individual features. Metabolite features with adjusted p-values less than 0.05 are considered differentially abundant and
metabolite features with adjusted p-values less than 0.4 are reported in the table.
